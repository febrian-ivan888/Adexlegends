ARENA

Be the better team in this 3v3 deathmatch

Show them what you can do with those weapons in this 3v3 team deathmatch mode. Each team will have unlimited loadout supply in order to achieve the first to 40 kills.

ARENA MAPS

BARREN

Situated in the middle of a desert, Barren is the top choice to show of your shooting skills. With little to no obstacle between the two teams, Barren highly reward players who just don’t miss their shot. The city center in the middle of the map also rewards brave legends who dare contest the loot and their inhabitants.

OKYOTA

The futuristic city of Okyota, home to Ace and all that came before her. The downtown offers multiple buildings with various terrains and veticality enabling even the weakest of legends to gain an upperhand as long as they are familiar with this city’s layout. Being a modern city, Okyota also provides rotating buffs every round to both teams.

INERTIA

What was once a testing site for the Phase Runner Technology has now turned into an arena where teams can test out their speed and flanking coordination. Equipped with a Phase Runner on each side of the map, teams can immediately go through it and throw immediate chaos to the arena. Phase runners also provide more attacking variety in each game.
