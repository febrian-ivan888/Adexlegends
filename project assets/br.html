PHONY PARADISE

This place wasn’t as big as it used to be, nor is it as beautiful. It came with the arrival of the legends initiative bringing entertainment and battles towards this land. With a size of 7x7 km, there is surely not enough room for all legends here, thus the need to adapt and survive. This paradise is surely not for everyone, but only for the best of legends.

CLIFFSIDE CHAOS

Highly unstable both geographically and situationally, Cliffside is the literal embodiment of chaos itself. Boasting a size of 10x10 km, there is plenty of loot for everyone, if you are willing to search deeper. Find secret hideouts within the outskirts of the map in search for higher tier loots, but remember, the red zone is always ready to take you out.
